    America and the Five Year Plan

    25 February 2017

    We are pleased to make available to you two recently released letters from the National Spiritual Assembly.

    The first, a cover letter addressed to the American Bahá’í community, is intended to introduce the second, a letter conveying the National Assembly’s thoughts on the current troubling state of affairs in our country and on our most effective response as Bahá’ís.

    Each of the two documents is presented in English, in PDF form. Translations of the second―the Assembly’s letter on “America and the Five Year Plan”―are offered in Persian and Spanish, also in PDF form.

    Universal House of Justice

    Supreme Body Greets Participants in Dedication of Mother Temple of South America

    14 October 2016

    We are pleased to make available to you electronically a message from the Universal House of Justice released today, October 14, and addressed to the friends gathered in Santiago for the dedication of the newly completed Bahá’í House of Worship of Chile, the Mother Temple of the South American continent.
    The message is provided in English and in French and Spanish translations. Additional translations will be posted as they become available.

    National Spiritual Assembly

    Avoidance of Partisan Politics Stressed

    10 March 2016

    We direct your attention to a letter from the National Spiritual Assembly stressing -- for all Bahá'ís – the importance of avoiding partisan politics.
    Note that the letter is supplemented by a message from the Universal House of Justice, addressed to the Bahá'ís of Iran and provided in English, Persian, and Spanish. The message, which takes up this same subject, is commended to your further study and reflection.
    All four of the documents are provided in PDF form for your ease of access and printing.
