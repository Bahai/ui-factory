Supreme Body Greets Participants in Dedication of Mother Temple of South America

14 October 2016

We are pleased to make available to you electronically a message from the Universal House of Justice released today, October 14, and addressed to the friends gathered in Santiago for the dedication of the newly completed Bahá’í House of Worship of Chile, the Mother Temple of the South American continent.
The message is provided in English and in French and Spanish translations. Additional translations will be posted as they become available.
