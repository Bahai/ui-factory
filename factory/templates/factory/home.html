National Spiritual Assembly Encourages Study of Three Major Letters

In the last year, the National Assembly has released three major letters― February 25 and December 8 of 2017 and January 31 of this year―focused on the current state of society and our appropriate response as Bahá’ís. Riḍván 2017 Annual Report

Avoidance of Partisan Politics Stressed

We direct your attention to a letter from the National Spiritual Assembly (download here) stressing – for all Bahá'ís – the importance of avoiding partisan politics.

Note that the letter is supplemented by a message from the Universal House of Justice, addressed to the Bahá'ís of Iran and provided in English, Persian, and Spanish. The message, which takes up this same subject, is commended to your further study and reflection.
