America and the Five Year Plan

25 February 2017

We are pleased to make available to you two recently released letters from the National Spiritual Assembly.

The first, a cover letter addressed to the American Bahá’í community, is intended to introduce the second, a letter conveying the National Assembly’s thoughts on the current troubling state of affairs in our country and on our most effective response as Bahá’ís.

Each of the two documents is presented in English, in PDF form. Translations of the second―the Assembly’s letter on “America and the Five Year Plan”―are offered in Persian and Spanish, also in PDF form.

Mr. Paul Lample, Member of the House of Justice, Elaborates on the New Five Year Plan

May 20, 2016


Transcript of Talk Given by Mr. Paul Lample at Bahá’í House of Worship

The National Spiritual Assembly is delighted to make available to the friends, many of whom have requested it, a full transcript of the talk given by Mr. Paul Lample, current member of the Universal House of Justice, at the Bahá’í House of Worship in Wilmette, on May 20 of this year.

The transcript, which is a little over 18 pages in length, can be accessed through the link immediately below. It can either be read online or downloaded to your own computer for long-term study and reflection.

Transcript of May 20 Talk by Mr. Paul Lample at the Bahá’í House of Worship

The talk also continues to be available in audio form through access provided elsewhere on this same first page of the national member services website.
