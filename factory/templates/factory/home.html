America and the Five Year Plan

25 February 2017

We are pleased to make available to you two recently released letters from the National Spiritual Assembly.

The first, a cover letter addressed to the American Bahá’í community, is intended to introduce the second, a letter conveying the National Assembly’s thoughts on the current troubling state of affairs in our country and on our most effective response as Bahá’ís.

Each of the two documents is presented in English, in PDF form. Translations of the second―the Assembly’s letter on “America and the Five Year Plan”―are offered in Persian and Spanish, also in PDF form.
